Key FAQs

1

Is the MBA (WX) program well-regarded by corporate and start-up firms?

Yes. The program enjoys high corporate recognition with the help of 82,000+ NMIMS CDOE alumni working in 8,000+ corporate firms. Besides, the program faculty are experienced and accomplished professionals who are well-connected in the business community, further cementing the industry recognition.

2

Will there be opportunities to engage with industry experts through guest seminars?

A major part of the 800+ hours of live interactive lectures (delivered online) is conducted by industry veterans & experts, as well as academicians. Students take part in discussion forums, and post their queries for addressing doubts with them, gaining valuable insights and networking opportunities.

3

Is MBA (WX) - Operations & Supply Chain program recognised by AICTE/UGC?

Yes, the MBA (WX) in Operations & Supply Chain program, offered in Online learning mode, is recognised by UGC. SVKM's NMIMS Deemed-to-be-University has a CGPA of 3.67 and an A++ NAAC rating. It has also earned the status of Category 1 University by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD).

4

How will MBA (WX) - Operations & Supply Chain Management program improve my career in Operations & Supply Chain?

The program, along with an intensive academic curriculum, will help you with experiential learning through the Capstone Project and the Industry Project. You'll also be able to build networking opportunities with potential employers, industry experts, and a vast alumni network for enhancing your career prospects.

5

Does the curriculum of MBA (WX) - Operations & Supply Chain ensure a good mix of managerial and technical skills?

Yes, the curriculum has a good mix of courses like Strategic Management, Finance, Business Communication, as well as technical subjects like Supply Chain Management, Logistics Management, and Total Quality Management to prepare you well for operations and supply chain management roles with dynamic business challenges.
