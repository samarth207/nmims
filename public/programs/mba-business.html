Frequently Asked Questions

Is NMIMS Online MBA in Business Management UGC approved?+

Yes. The NMIMS Online MBA in Business Management is UGC and DEB approved, offered by NMIMS NGA-SCE.

Who should choose MBA in Business Management vs a specialised MBA?+

Business Management is ideal for professionals who work across multiple functions, run their own businesses, or aspire to general management roles such as GM, COO, or CEO. If you want deep domain expertise, a specialised MBA (Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations) may be better.

Can I pursue this MBA while working full-time?+

Yes. The program is fully online with live sessions and recorded lectures, designed specifically for working professionals.

What is the total program fee?+

Total fees start from ₹1.5 Lakhs with EMI options available. Contact our admissions team for the latest fee structure.
