Frequently Asked Questions

Is NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing UGC approved?+

Yes. The NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management is offered by NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education (NGA-SCE) and is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Distance Education Bureau (DEB).

Is there an entrance exam for this program?+

No. NMIMS Online MBA in Marketing Management does not require any entrance exam such as CAT, MAT, or GMAT. Admission is based on graduation marks and document verification.

What is the total fee for the Online MBA in Marketing?+

The total program fee starts from ₹1.5 Lakhs. EMI options are available. Contact our admissions team for the latest fee structure and scholarships.

Can I pursue this MBA while working full-time?+

Absolutely. The NMIMS Online MBA is specifically designed for working professionals. Classes are conducted through live sessions and recorded lectures, allowing you to study at your own pace without leaving your job.

What are the career prospects after this MBA?+

Graduates can pursue roles such as Brand Manager, Digital Marketing Manager, Product Manager, Marketing Analytics Manager, and more. NMIMS provides placement support through 500+ hiring partners.
