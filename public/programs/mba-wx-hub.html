Eligibility Criteria

To apply for this program, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Bachelor's Degree (10+2+3) in any discipline from recognised University or an equivalent degree recognised by Association of Indian Universities (AIU)
  • Minimum 55% marks at Graduation Level (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD)
  • 3+ years of Work Experience required

Registration Process

Here's a step-by-step registration process for enrolling into this program.

STEP 1

Application

Register online at nmimsonlineuniversity.com. Fill up the Application Form online and pay the mentioned application fee.

STEP 2

Document Submission

Candidate can upload the Gazette Attested photocopies of their documents including Academic Documents, Work Experience Document, Photo Identity, and Passport Size Photograph.

STEP 3

Personal Interview

Students enrolling in the MBA (WX) program will have to undergo a Personal Interview.

STEP 4

Fee Payment

Selected candidates are required to make part fee payment of Rs. 40,000 within 7 days of issuance of the offer letter for seat reservation. Remaining program fee to be paid within 1 month of the offer letter date.

STEP 5

Confirmation

Upon completion of the above-mentioned steps and when the submitted documents, Student verification and payments are approved by the University, your admission will be confirmed, and a 'student number' will be issued to you.

Key FAQs

1

What is a NMIMS Regional Office and NMAT / NPAT Center?

−

To ensure effective support, NMIMS University has set up Regional Offices across different locations in India. These offices act as the local point of contact for students within that area. These offices also conduct NMAT & NPAT examinations. To get more information on our Regional Offices, visit: https://nmimsonlineuniversity.com/contact-us/

2

What Academic help will I receive after registering for the program?

+

After registering for any program a student will receive semester-wise self learning material (if applicable), recordings of live sessions, course presentations, session presentations and any additional reading material as shared by faculty if any.

3

Do I have to appear for a written assessment for the MBA (WX) program?

+

No written assessments are required. You only need to match the eligibility criteria mentioned above. There will, however, be a personal interview after your documents have been reviewed.

4

Do I have to be a working professional to apply for the MBA (WX) program?

+

Yes, you need to have a minimum of 3 years of work experience. Refer to the eligibility criteria mentioned above.

5

How to attend live lectures in the MBA (WX) program?

+

To attend live lectures, go to: Student Portal → Academic Calendar → Click on the scheduled Session.
