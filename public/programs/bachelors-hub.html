Eligibility Criteria

To apply for this program, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • HSC (10+2) in any discipline from a recognised board
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD)

Key FAQs

1

I dropped out of my degree over two years ago. Am I still eligible to apply?

−

Yes, you can still apply, as long as you fulfil the eligibility criteria specified above.

2

Will I get an internship during or after completing bachelor's program?

+

Currently, there is no internship offered in the program.

3

Do you prefer BBA & B.Com graduates for Diploma in Financial Management program?

+

While graduates from BBA or B.Com have an advantage with a foundational knowledge of finance, the program is open to all graduates aiming to build or enhance their financial expertise.

4

What happens if a student clears all the subjects but fails to submit project in the Bachelor's program?

+

Non-submission of project/failure in project at the end of program validity will lead to non-completion of the enrolled Program.
