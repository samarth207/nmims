Frequently Asked Questions

Is NMIMS Online MBA in Finance UGC approved?+

Yes. The NMIMS Online MBA in Financial Management is UGC and DEB approved, offered by NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education (NGA-SCE).

Is there an entrance exam for admission?+

No entrance exam is required. Admission is based on graduation marks and document verification.

What is the total program fee?+

Total fees start from ₹1.5 Lakhs. EMI options are available. Contact us for the latest fee structure.

Can working professionals pursue this MBA?+

Yes. The program is 100% online with live sessions and recorded lectures, making it ideal for working finance professionals.
